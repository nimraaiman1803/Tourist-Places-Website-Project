RAMOJI FILM CITY

❮ ❯

One of the best ways to understand the convoluted workings of the film industry is to make a visit to the Ramoji Film City. This place seems almost surreal with its wide collection of gardens and sets that have your interests piqued. Further, the place also hosts a number of shows to keeps the guests entertained. One of the must-visit Hyderabad sightseeing places, this place is an experience in itself.

  • Location: Hayathnagar Mandal
  • Timings: 9 am - 5:30 pm
  • Entry Fee: INR 800- INR 1799 with varying packages.

Ramoji Film City is one of Hyderabad major tourist attractions. Located roughly 28 kilometres from Hyderabad, this theme park is visited by people of all ages, as there are lots of activities open to everyone. A place ideal for all Bollywood and Film lovers from all over India! Established in 1991 by Ramoji Rao, head of the Ramoji party, Ramoji Film City is a spectacular getaway outside Hyderabad city. Spread over 2,500 acres and the film city holds a place in the Guinness Book of World Records as the world largest film studio complex. Ramoji Film City can support 20 film units at one go and has a range of shooting locations including London Street, Hollywood signage, Japanese Gardens, airport, hospital, landscapes, and laboratories. Being one of Hyderabads most popular places to visit, one can reach Ramoji Film City by tour buses or also public buses that leave the city regularly.

Adventure Sports: Feel the adrenaline rush of playing several action sports such as Bungee ejection, Rugby, swinging and sliding around the loop. Also, you can go for many other sports such as basketball, cricket, etc. You will have a great time playing those games with your friends and family.

Studio Tour If you are an ardent lover of nature you would love to enjoy the popular Studio Tour. You can also visit the iconic Askari Garden, Japanese Garden, Mughal Garden, Sun Fountain Garden, Angels Fountain Garden, Sanctuary Garden as a part of this tour. Experiencing the verdant landscape with distinctive characteristics all around is truly wonderful.

Movie Magic Park At the Ramoji Film Citys Movie Magic Park, you can enjoy many magical moments in which you can experience earthquake tremors, enjoy free fall simulation experience, amazing acoustic effects, Filmi Duniya and Action Studio-an amazing experience for film lovers.

Wings-The Bird Park If you are an avid bird watcher, then this site is a must-visit! A vast expanse of green scenery with chirping birds creating a melodious atmosphere.

Kids Park If you and your children are visiting Ramoji Film City, you must visit the popular Kids Park, where you can enjoy a series of fun activities and rides such as Thrillville Rides, Wonderville, Dadajinns Ark, Borasura, Ballerina Fount, Enthraller, Timberland and many more. Children would love to enjoy these shows and rides.
